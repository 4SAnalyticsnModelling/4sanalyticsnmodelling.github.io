Projects:

HyperLayer Data Management and Machine Learning for Smart Agriculture

    Analyzing multilayer spectral imageries, and field measurements of crop yields and soil test results to predict yields and yield limiting factors using machine learning. The outcomes of this project will support smart and precision agriculture in Alberta. This project is led by the Olds College Centre for Innovation (OCCI) in collaboration with prominent industry partners in the field of agricultural automation and artificial intelligence.

A Review of On-Farm Ammonia Production and Associated Carbon Offsets in Alberta

    Economic and environmental pre-feasibility review of an alternative ammonia production system in Alberta for an early stage company. The project is led by the Olds College Centre for Innovation (OCCI).

Appraisal of a Novel Vertical Farming Horticultural Production System: A Research Proposal

    Drafting a funding proposal for an early stage company to evaluate feasibility of their novel vertical farming system in Alberta. The project is led by the Olds College Centre for Innovation (OCCI).

Towards a Fire Early Warning System for Indonesia (ToFEWSI)

    Machine learning, deep learning and process-based modelling to devise an early wildfire forecasting tool for Riau province of Indonesia. It's a multinational collaboration led by Swansea University, UK. The project is funded through the UK’s National Environment Research Council – Newton Fund on behalf of UK Research & Innovation and Indonesia Endowment Fund for Education and the Indonesian Science Fund.
